A FEW ABOUT US

Welcome to Stela Apartments, your ideal home away from home in the picturesque coastal town of Ksamil, Albania. Located just 600 meters from the breathtaking Ksamil Beach, our accommodation seamlessly combines comfort, convenience, and serenity.

Whether you're planning a family vacation, a romantic escape, or a solo retreat, Stela Apartments has everything you need for an unforgettable stay.
