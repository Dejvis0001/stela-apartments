WELCOME TO

STELA APARTMENTS

Providing garden views and a garden, Stela Apartments features accommodation conveniently situated in Ksamil, within a short distance of Ksamil Beach, Sunset Beach and Coco Beach. It is located 3.4 km from Butrint National Park and offers full-day security. The guest house provides rooms with air conditioning, free private parking and free WiFi.

Each unit features a balcony, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, a dining area and a flat-screen TV, while the private bathroom includes a bidet and a hair dryer. At the guest house, each unit is equipped with bed linen and towels. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 93 km from the guest house.
