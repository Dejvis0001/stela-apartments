About Us!

Discover Stela Apartments, your serene oasis in Ksamil. Located just steps from Ksamil Beach, Sunset Beach, and Coco Beach, our guest house offers the perfect escape.

Situated 3.4 kilometers from Butrint National Park, explore history and nature at your doorstep.Enjoy peace of mind with 24/7 security, air-conditioned rooms, and free parking. Each unit features a private balcony, fully equipped kitchen, and flat-screen TV.

Refresh in the modern bathroom with a bidet and hairdryer, and rest easy with fresh linens and towels.Corfu International Airport is just 93 kilometers away, ensuring effortless travel connections. Experience comfort and convenience at Stela Apartments.
